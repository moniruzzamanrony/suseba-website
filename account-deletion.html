Account Deletion

At Suseba, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your account.

If you wish to delete your account, you can do it directly from the app by following this path:

Profile → Delete This Account

Once you confirm the deletion, your account and associated data will be permanently removed from our system.

If you face any issues, feel free to contact our support team.
